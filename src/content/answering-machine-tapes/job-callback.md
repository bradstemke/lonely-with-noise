This message represents a moment of possibility - someone's potential new job, frozen in time on magnetic tape. We'll never know if they got the job, if they even called back, or where they are now.

The formality of the message, the careful enunciation, and the slightly robotic tone of professional courtesy all feel like artifacts from another era of workplace communication.
